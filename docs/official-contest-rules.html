4. Contest period

Each Contest displays its own start and end times in the App. Entries are accepted from the Contest's open time until it locks or ends, as shown on the Contest's detail screen.

5. How winners are determined

Contests are determined predominantly by skill, not chance. Each entrant begins with the same simulated starting bankroll and trades the same available markets using identical, deterministic prices. Final standings are ranked by highest simulated portfolio equity at the Contest's end. In the event of a tie, the entrant who reached the tied equity earliest wins. The Sponsor's determination of winners is final.

No prize is transferable and no cash or other substitution is offered except at the Sponsor's discretion. The odds of winning a prize depend on the number and skill of eligible entrants. Where a Contest awards only experience points ("XP"), XP has no cash value and is not redeemable for money.
