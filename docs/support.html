Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Profile → Delete account, and confirm. This permanently removes your account and all associated data (profile, simulated trades, public leaderboard entry, and avatar). Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

How do I report or block another user?

On a trader's profile or in the Top Traders list, tap the ••• menu and choose Report content or Block user. Blocking immediately hides that user from your feeds and notifies us. You can manage blocked users under Profile → Blocked users.

Is my data shared?

Your handle, avatar, and simulated scores appear on public leaderboards so you can compete with other players. Your email address is never shown publicly. See our Privacy Policy for details.
